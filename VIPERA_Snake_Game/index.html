How to play

  • Desktop: Arrow keys or WASD to steer. Space to pause. F for fullscreen.
  • Mobile / tablet: Use the on-screen D-pad, or swipe anywhere on the board.
  • Guide Vipera to each golden orb — every bite grows your length and your score.
  • Avoid colliding with your own tail. Choose Easy, Medium, or Hard for different speeds.
  • Your best score is saved automatically on this device.
